About Me

More than just code

Born and raised in the Bay Area, I've always been surrounded by creativity, innovation, and community. I’m a builder by nature — whether it’s crafting a project, solving a problem, or bringing ideas to life. After running my own videography business and spending years immersed in music production, I transitioned into full-stack web development, bringing with me a deep understanding of collaboration, leadership, and real-world problem-solving.

I’m not a kid fresh out of college; I’m a communicator who values people, clear direction, and thoughtful execution. With two solid years of coding experience and 42 years of life experience, I approach every project with curiosity, humility, and a focus on the big picture — always asking why we’re building something, not just how. I care about creating meaningful tools, keeping things human, and making the work itself enjoyable.
